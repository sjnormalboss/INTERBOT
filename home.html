What is InterHackBot?

InterHackBot is a Discord bot specifically designed for online hackathons.
Use our many features to effciently work and enjoy your time in the hackathon!

Features

Time Zone Converter

Are your teammates from all over the world? Do you get confused when scheduling a meeting with them? No worries! With this feature set up, you can easily understand different time zone by triggering the command.

Language Converter

Are your teammates extremely bright in coding, but don't speak the same language? The language feature helps you convert over 75+ languages including French, Spanish, and more! You can get ahead of the competition with the help of your smart teammate with the help of InterHackBot.

Hackathon Timer & Reminder

Want to work more efficiently with a Pomodoro timer? InterHackBot can totally help you with that! This feature also includes a shortcut to send your teammate a message after a certain time. For example, remind them to start working after 10 minutes!

Personalized ID Card

For those who are still in need of a team and those who would like to proudly display their stats this feature is for you! You can add various stats including your experience level all the way to your hobbies. Others who see that you are still looking for teammates can reach out to you, and you can get started right away!

Hackathon Infomation

With this feature admins will be prompted to enter crucial hackathon information in which participants can easily access and understand upon triggering the command.
